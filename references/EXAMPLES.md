## Beispiele (Session-Notation)

Diese Beispiele sind bewusst **kurz genug zum Copy/Paste** und zeigen das Pattern:
`[...]` = Regie/Produktion • normale Zeilen = Lyrics/Vocals.

---

## Beispiel 1: Intimer „Small Room“ Song (Mid-Detail)

```text
[Title: "Stay Awhile"]
[Genre/Style: late-night soul / minimal jazz-pop]
[Tempo: 86 BPM | Swing: light]
[Key: A minor]
[Drums: soft snare + light kick pulse; hats minimal]
[Bass: upright 2-bar ostinato; constant anchor]
[Instruments: muted guitar comping | Rhodes chords | subtle sax fills late]
[Space/Texture: small room | tape hiss low | close mic lead]
[Feel: intimate | steady | sincere]

[Intro — set tone]
[bass 2-bar riff loops | muted guitar comping | tape hiss]
[Vocal: spoken, close mic, intimate]
Don’t go… not tonight.

[Verse 1 — restrained pocket]
[Bars 1–4: bass + soft snare + kick only]
When the night gets heavy and the streetlights blur,
I’m holding my breath like a quiet rumor.

[Bars 5–8: add gentle Rhodes chords | same groove]
I don’t need a miracle, I don’t need a sign,
Just sit in the silence and let it unwind.

[Pre — small lift | breath space]
[background: soft wordless hum | cymbal shimmer]
Say you’ll stay a while,
That’s enough to breathe.

[Hook — mantra hook | tight + serious]
[Harmonies: low harmony on last word each line]
[On word: "spiral" (Hook line 2): drop hats for 1 beat]
Stay a while, stay a while,
When the walls close in, don’t let me spiral.
Hold the line, hold the line,
If I lose my words, just stay a while.

[Bridge — intimate breakdown]
[4 bars: bass + guitar + Rhodes only | drums drop]
[Vocal: breathy; close mic]
If you hear me shaking, don’t ask why—
Just keep your hand near mine.

[Final Hook — final lift]
[drums return | subtle sax fills between lines | slightly fuller stack]
Stay a while, stay a while,
When the walls close in, don’t let me spiral.
Hold the line, hold the line,
If I lose my words, just stay a while.

[Outro — fade]
[drums mute last 2 bars | bass riff fade | tape fade]
```

## Beispiel 2: New-Wave Rap / Modern Hip-Hop (Full-Detail, „Micro-Switch“)

```text
[Title: "Deep End Nights"]
[Genre/Style: new-wave rap / modern hip-hop w/ bounce]
[Key: D minor]
[Tempo: 80 BPM | Swing: 56%]
[Drums: tight kit; crisp snare/clap on 2&4; swung 1/8 hats w/ 1/16 flurries; triplet bursts sparingly]
[Bass: 808 long sustain; glide up 2 semitones into each Hook; slides on bars 4 & 8]
[Instruments: staccato violin chops (call) | airy panflute (response) | lush harp arps | glassy pad bed]
[Atmos: vinyl crackle low | distant crowd/sea breeze very low | subtle room tone]
[Feel: nocturnal | triumphant | vibey]

[Intro — set the scene]
[Filter: low-pass 300→8k over 8 bars; 808 muted early; hats ghosted]
[panflute motif mono→wide 120% by bar 7]
[Reverse vox tail: 1/2 bar into Hook]
(beat breathes)

[Hook — pocket + detail]
[Kick: busier bars 3–4 & 7–8; ghost kick on “&” of 3]
[violin chops off-beat | panflute ad-libs low L/R]
[FX: tape-stop 1 beat on bar 8 | backspin short into bar 9]
City on my shoulders but I’m moving light,
Hands on the wheel and my future bright.
Step-by-step, I don’t show the doubt,
If they want the wave— I’m the deep end now.

[Verse 1 — micro-switch]
[Micro-switch: Bars 7–8 (of this Verse): low-pass 10k→1k; hats mute bar 8]
Took a long road just to level up,
Quiet wins, never needed luck.
I don’t talk big, I just do it clean,
New night, same spark, same movie scene.

[Pre — riser]
[Drop kick; clap verb-throw one-shot]
[Reverse vox tail: 1/2 bar; 808 swell −∞→−6 dB]
Turn the pressure into power— watch it click.

[Hook — repeat + lift]
[Add higher harmony on line 2 | slightly wider]
[FX: tape-stop 1 beat on bar 8]
City on my shoulders but I’m moving light,
Hands on the wheel and my future bright.
Step-by-step, I don’t show the doubt,
If they want the wave— I’m the deep end now.

[Bridge — contrast]
[Halftime drums 4–8 bars; pull hats; pad/choir “oohs” low]
[Vocal: slightly more melodic; ad-libs sparse]
Hold that frame— don’t blink,
Let it land— let it sink.

[Final Hook — final lift]
[Return to original pocket; fuller stack; subtle fills]
City on my shoulders but I’m moving light,
Hands on the wheel and my future bright.
Step-by-step, I don’t show the doubt,
If they want the wave— I’m the deep end now.

[Outro — let it breathe]
[drums mute last 2 bars; delay tail 3s; vinyl pop end]
```
